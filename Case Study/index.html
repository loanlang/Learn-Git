Delivery Rates

We Are Punctual

We know how important it is to receive your cake on time for your birthday or wedding celebrations. Timely deliveries make for high customer and client satisfaction. Receiving your cakes on time will make for a pleasant celebration process and we do not want to keep you waiting.

Responsible Cake Delivery Drivers
